About Us

Dome Vet Center is a leading provider of services in the Animal Healthcare industry. Our mission is to help our customers solve their pets' health issues, maintain and improve their pets' health through the use of cutting-edge technology. We pride ourselves on our ability to deliver customized solutions that meet the specific needs of each of our clients.
Dome Vet Center was founded in 2020 by a veterinarian who saw an opportunity to bring their expertise to the market. Since then, we have grown significantly, launching a range of successful services in Batam City.

Our Services

Pet Shop

Our shop is filled with a wide variety of high-quality products for your furry friends, including food, toys, bedding, and other supplies.

Pet Clinic

Our clinic offers a wide range of services, including routine check-ups, vaccinations, surgery, dental care, and more. We also are stocked with the medications and supplies your pet may need.

Pet Grooming

Our team of experienced groomers is trained to handle a wide range of breeds and sizes, and we use only the best products to ensure that your pet is comfortable and well-cared for.
